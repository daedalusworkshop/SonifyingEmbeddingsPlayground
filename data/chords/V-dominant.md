## Contrasting with Neighbors
Unlike IV, which yearns upward with peace, V strains forward with urgency. Unlike I, which is resolved, V is the most unresolved chord in the key — it almost physically requires the ear to continue. Its tension is horizontal (forward) rather than vertical (upward).
