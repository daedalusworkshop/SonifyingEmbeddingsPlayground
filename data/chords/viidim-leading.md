## Contrasting with Neighbors
Unlike V, whose tension is anticipatory and energized, vii°'s tension is destabilizing and dark — it doesn't feel like preparation, it feels like crisis. Unlike iii, which hovers in gentle ambiguity, vii° is emphatically, urgently unresolved. It is the least stable chord in the key.
